Sustainability

Churchill is a climate neutral beverage. In other words, we offset all product-related emissions! This amounts to an offset of 330g per bottle.

We support the Global Goals for Sustainable Development initiative and reached the carbon neutral certification by supporting the Plastic Bank Project. Waste collectors in Haiti, Indonesia, and the Philippines can earn their income by collecting waste and exchanging it at the local Plastic Bank branches for money, food, drinking water, mobile phone credit, cooking oil or even school fees. The exchange value is greater than the actual market value of plastic, meaning that people there really can live from it.

Drink a bottle, make a difference.
