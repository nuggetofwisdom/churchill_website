Churchill

Water is essential for our health, yet most of us still struggle to stay hydrated. We chose distilled sparkling water as the foundation of our Recovery Soda and enriched it with all-natural ingredients that will allow you to hydrate more efficiently.

Apart from nutritious fruits and herbs, CHURCHILL contains salt. Contrary to popular belief, salt is actually a hydration agent. It is an essential mineral that helps you hydrate more efficiently. Our Recovery Soda relies on this secret ingredient.

To increase health benefits, we use rose crystal salt which is mined at the foothills of the Himalayan Mountains. It offers 80 more minerals than regular salt, promotes increased absorption of nutrients, and helps to balance electrolytes.

On top of that, we understand that an excess of salt may impact your health. Therefore we also made sure to add a healthy amount of salt to optimize its effect. As a result Churchill offers neither to much, nor to little salt to keep you perfectly hydrated.

Mint

While mint is particularly known for its cooling sensation, it also has proven health benefits. CHURCHILL's garden mint is rich in nutrients, with high amounts of Vitamin A and Iron that allow your body to construct oxygen transport proteins. These proteins supply our cells with oxygen.

Moreover, mint is a strong source of antioxidants which help protect your body from oxidative stress - and who doesn’t love the taste of fresh mint?

Rose
Crystal Salt

You may have heard someone say that salt is bad for you. Let’s debunk that myth.

Our salt, mined from the foothills of the Himalayan mountains contains more than 80 different minerals.

Rose crystal salt is the best of its kind. Unlike table salt, rose crystal salt is naturally rich in iodine and has far lower sodium levels.

When water and salt are mixed into a drink, the negative ions of the water molecules combine with the positive ions of salt. Through this process, the molecules are electrically charged and thus can easily be absorbed by the body.

Story

Sustainability

Churchill is a climate neutral beverage. In other words, we offset all product related emissions! This amounts to an offset of 330g per bottle.

We support the Global Goals for Sustainable Development initiative and reached the carbon neutral certification by supporting the Plastic Bank project.

Waste collectors in Haiti, Indonesia and the Philippines can earn their income that way. Everybody there can collect waste and exchange it at the local Plastic Bank branches for money, food, drinking water, mobile phone credit, cooking oil or even school fees.

The exchange value is greater than the actual market value of plastic, meaning that people there really can live from it.
