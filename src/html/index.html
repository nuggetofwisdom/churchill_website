functionality

Most of us experience dehydration on a daily basis. The modern environment conspires to drain ever more water from our bodies. Keeping your water levels constant is a challenge for most people. Salt is crucial as it is a essential chemical for cellular function. (This was already understood by the romans, who partly paid their soldiers in salt.)

Churchill relies on this basic ingridient of hydration and tries to allow you to do more with less water. Our heightend Salt content allows the water to move from the outside to the inside of your cells. Here the water is critically needed to fuel all cell functions. Churchill contains, next to nutritious fruits and herbs, all natural Himalyan Salt which has extra minerals too add to your diet.

We understand that to much Salt has an impact on your health. For this reason, we carefully chose our dosis to to optimize the salt effect and offer not to much, but not to little salt for you.
